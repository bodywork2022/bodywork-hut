About Me

Terence Hitchens

Hi I am Terence, a sports massage therapist and owner of Bodywork Hut. I have a vast range of experience with high level athletes, hyrox competitors, footballers and bodybuilders.

That does not mean sports massage is just for athletes.....far from it! I specialise in helping people with their everyday pain, whether that is lower back pain from driving, hip pain while jogging or a shoulder pain when you wake up, I can help.

About Company

Bodywork Hut

Many people look for "a sports massage near me" but we bring the sports massage to you! We offer mobile sports massage services from our base in Sheffield I started the business to help people! I left a background in pharma behind to pursue my passion in health and fitness. In addition to our focus on occupational issues we have worked with a wide range of athletes from Hyrox competitors, high level triple and long jump athletes, footballers and more! For those who prefer to travel to a treatment room, we have recently set up inside Crossfit Braze Gym in Kelham Island, Sheffield.
